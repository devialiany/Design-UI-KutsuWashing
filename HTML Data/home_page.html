Service Type:

Full Clean Service is cleaning all parts of the shoe in detail starting from midsole, outsole, insole, upper and lace.

After cleaning, the shoes will be repainted so that the faded shoe color can return to be normal again.

Unyellowing is a technique of removing the yellow color that usually occurs in the midsole. The longer use of shoes, the color of the original midsole will turn yellow due to frequent exposure to sunlight.

So, This service is perfect for you who want to make old shoes like new again.

Repaint is a coloring technique for shoes that follows the initial color of the shoe which has previously faded due to usage.


Outercleaning is the technique of washing shoes only on the outside of the shoe and the bottom of the shoe sole to make it look clean and fresh. outercleaning is specifically for those who are in a hurry and want fast results, because outercleaning only takes 1 hour.
